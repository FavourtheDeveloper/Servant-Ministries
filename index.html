COMMENT ON PROPHECY

FORCEFULLY ADVANCING THE KINGDOM (Matthew 11:12 ISV)

We are sent out, appointed and anointed to declare and encourage others in the pursuit of the good, pleasing and perfect will of GOD for their lives; to pray unceasingly to our FATHER, "Your Kingdom come, Your will be done"...

We do this conscious of the fact that the outworkings and frailties of humanity do not always lend themselves to the full accomplishment of His perfect will.

We however remain undettered as we keep on praying pursuing and prophesying His will in our lives and the lives of those we are sent to.

"And from the days of John the Baptist until now the kingdom of heaven suffereth violence, and men of violence take it by force" Matthew 11vs12
